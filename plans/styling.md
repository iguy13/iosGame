# Styling Guide

## Overall Vibe
- Energetic, bold, fun
- Rural countryside meets motorsports
- Open road attitude

## Color Palette
| Color | Hex | Usage |
|-------|-----|-------|
| Primary Black | `#1A1A1A` | Backgrounds, headers |
| Accent Orange | `#FF6B00` | Buttons, highlights, active states |
| White | `#FFFFFF` | Text, icons |
| Dark Gray | `#2D2D2D` | Cards, panels |
| Light Gray | `#B0B0B0` | Secondary text, borders |
| Red | `#E53935` | Brake indicator, warnings |
| Green | `#43A047` | Go / gas indicator, success |

## Typography
- **Headings**: Bold, uppercase, aggressive font (e.g., Bebas Neue or Impact style)
- **Body text**: Clean sans-serif (e.g., Roboto, Open Sans)
- **HUD numbers**: Monospace or digital-style font for speedometer/timer

## UI Elements

### Buttons
- Rounded rectangle with slight bevel
- Orange accent for primary actions
- Dark gray for secondary actions
- Press animation: slight scale down + color darken

### Cards (Character / Vehicle Select)
- Dark background with subtle border
- Glow effect on selected card (orange border)
- Vehicle stats shown as horizontal bar graphs

### Menus
- Semi-transparent dark overlay backgrounds
- Slide-in animations for panels
- Smooth transitions between screens

## In-Game Styling

### Speedometer
- Circular gauge, dark face with orange needle
- Digital speed readout in center (MPH)
- Subtle glow effect

### Minimap
- Rounded square, semi-transparent background
- Player icon as arrow showing direction
- Road outlines in light gray

### Timer
- Digital clock style font
- White text, subtle drop shadow
- Flashes red in last 10 seconds (timed modes)

## Environment Styling
- Rural/country setting — dirt roads, fields, lakes, forests
- Snow areas for snowmobile zones
- Water areas for potential boat zones
- Ramps, jumps, and obstacles scattered around
- Time of day: golden hour / afternoon lighting for warm feel
- Dust/snow particle effects when driving off-road
